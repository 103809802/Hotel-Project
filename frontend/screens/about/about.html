About Me

My name is Thac, I am an ex Hotel Executive Director and a Hospitality Expert, and my objective is to share practical knowledge and real-world experience to help students develop professional skills, understand industry standards, and build successful careers in the hospitality field.

Professional Experience

  • Vice Director of Operations – Muong Thanh Group (2018–2024): Oversaw hotel operations, service quality, cost efficiency, and staff training.
  • General Manager – Muong Thanh Luxury Bac Ninh (2016–2018): Led a top-performing 5-star hotel; built strong teams and B2B partnerships.
  • Director of Accommodation Services – Muong Thanh Group (2013–2015): Managed training and recruitment for Front Office, Housekeeping, and Guest Relations.
  • Front Office Supervisor – Hanoi Horison, Pullman, Daewoo Hotel (1997–2012): Supervised front office operations at international 5-star hotels.
